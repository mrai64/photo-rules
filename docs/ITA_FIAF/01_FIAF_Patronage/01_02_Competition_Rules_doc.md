# 1.2. Competition Rules

Into the doc, printed or digitally published and publically downloadable, must be present:

1. Contest denomination
2. Contest organizer denomination and address
3. E-mail and cell number of contest chairman / president
4. received Patronage Code (or raccomandation code, if)
5. FIAF mark (material and terms of use are in fiaf site)[link](https://fiaf.net/dipartimento-comunicazione/disciplinare-immagine-logo-fiaf/)
6. Jury member list, with: first_name, last_name, and facultative: distinctions codes, roles in respective organization, organization;
calendar days for jury works (see [1.3.](#) )
7. Calendar date:  
- ending date for send works;  
- jury reunion date;  
- result revelation day (upto 10 day after jury reunion);  
- salon exposition info and date (for printed contest min 2 day exposition);  
- web exposition (min 90 day for web);  
- projection date info for digital contests;  
- info about Award ceremony (how and when);  
- for printed contest resend originals upto 90 day after award ceremony;  
- send digital catalogues upto 45 day for digital contests;  
- send printed catalogues upto 90 day after award ceremony.  
Unjustified extensions of the deadlines originally communicated **are not permitted.**  
From the end date of receipt of photographs and the start date of the exhibition, screening, publication on the Web or
the award ceremony cannot take longer than 90 days.
8. Detailed Award List (see [1.4.](#) )
9. Participation fees (see [1.7.](#) )
10. Rule of Entry (see [1.5.](#) ).
